# Reception TV (Public Display) Setup Guide ‰ªó

The **Reception TV** feature provides a real-time, high-visibility dashboard for the clinic waiting area. It displays the current patient being served, the upcoming queue, and provides automated voice announcements.

## 1. Prerequisites ‰ªó

- **Account Role**: `CLINIC_ADMIN`, `DOCTOR`, `RECEPTIONIST`, or `NURSE`.
- **Displays**: A large-screen TV or monitor (Recommended: 1080p or 4K resolution).
- **Connection**: Laptop, Tablet, or PC connected to the display.

## 2. Accessing the Display ‰ªó

1. Log in to the **Medico Dashboard** (Web or Mobile).
2. Navigate to your **Operational Terminal** (for Doctors) or **Institutional Command** (for Clinics).
3. Select **RECEPTION TV** (or **Public Display**).
4. The display will automatically synchronize with your live queue.

## 3. Hardware Setup Options ‰ªó

### Option A: Wired (Recommended) ‰ªó
1. Connect a laptop or PC to the TV via **HDMI**.
2. Open the Medico Web App in a browser (Chrome/Edge/Safari).
3. Open the Reception TV screen and press `F11` (or your browser's Fullscreen shortcut).

### Option B: Wireless Casting ‰ªó
1. Use a Tablet or Smartphone to open the Medico App.
2. Use **AirPlay** (iOS/Mac) or **Chromecast** (Android/Windows) to mirror your screen to the TV.
3. Ensure the device remains powered and the screen is set to landscape.

### Option C: Smart TV Browser ‰ªó
1. Open the built-in web browser on your Smart TV.
2. Log in to the Medico portal.
3. Navigate directly to the Reception TV URL.

## 4. Key Features ‰ªó

- **Dynamic Queues**: Automatically updates as patients are checked in or called.
- **Voice Announcements**: Uses Text-to-Speech to announce: *"Now calling [Patient Name]. Please proceed to [Doctor Name/Room]."*
- **Emergency Overlay**: Instantly broadcast emergency protocols to all patients in the waiting area.
- **Clock & Date**: High-visibility time keeping for patients.

## 5. Optimization & Troubleshooting ‰ªó

- **Audio Alerts**: If voice announcements are not playing, ensure you have clicked anywhere on the screen once to "activate" the browser's audio permissions.
- **Screen Sleeping**: Set your device's "Display Sleep" or "Screen Saver" to **Never** to avoid the TV turning off.
- **Connectivity**: The display polls for changes every 5 seconds. Ensure a stable internet connection.
